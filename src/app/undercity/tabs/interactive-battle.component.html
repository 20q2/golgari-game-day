How to fight

Each round, read the foe’s telegraphed intent (the icon over its head) and pick the stance that beats it:

  • Aggress beats Feint
  • bolt Feint beats Guard
  • shield Guard beats Aggress

Win the read and you land the big hit; guess wrong and you eat one. Your ATK/DEF/SPD (beside each fighter) set how hard the blows land.

Scry reveals the true intent (bosses bluff). Flee bails a losing fight. Combat items fire on your next stance.
